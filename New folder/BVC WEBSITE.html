3 Decades of Excellence

Crafting Excellence:
BVC Exports is a leading manufacturer of traditional Indian snacks, renowned for our high-quality Appalam. With 30 years of expertise, we ensure consistent excellence in every batch.

Connecting Cultures:
We’ve expanded beyond manufacturing to become a trusted exporter of a wide range of food products, taking the taste of India to worldwide markets.

Our History & Milestones
